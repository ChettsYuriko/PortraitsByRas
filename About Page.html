About Us

Portraits by RAS Digital is a photography studio that specializes in capturing personalities
and memories through creative portraits.

With her vast experience from working at RAS
Digital — their family business of a photo and
video coverage team for events — and
individual freelance projects, she was able to
build a diverse portfolio and this creative space.

With our dedicated team, Portraits by RAS Digital is committed to capturing the
unique personalities of each of our clients. We guarantee that every photoshoot will
be a fun and special experience for you.
